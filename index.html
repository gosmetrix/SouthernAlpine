Welcome to Stanwix Australia

Times have changed and the market place is only going to get more competitive. Gaining new customers through emerging avenues is becoming more reliant on technology, takes time and continually increases costs. This means that organisations are struggling to find ways to attract new customers. STANWIX believes that most organisations have the information at arms-length, however, due to lack of understanding or poor translation of the communication, they fail to take action. STANWIX simplifies the communication process by working closely with your senior executives (CXO) to provide easy-to-read reporting based on the selection criteria specified by your organisation. STANWIX provides organisations with visual outcomes that assist you in making those important decisions.

WHEN DO YOU NEED STANWIX bUSINESS iNTELLIGENCE

In today’s busy market place, customers are equipped with smartphones and tablets, so they can buy anywhere at any time. They make decisions based on their interaction with social networks, and they no longer need to go to a store and nor do they want to. Everything is just a click away. STANWIX can provide you with the business intelligence that can help you today. You may not know it yet, but you could be sitting on a pot of gold within your organization. However, if you can’t translate this important information and use it to make effective business decisions, then you are giving away your customers. Customers make decisions quickly and their loyalty is twice as hard to obtain once lost. STANWIX can prevent that from happening.
